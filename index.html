About Us

We are a well established Sichuan Restaurant in the Seattle area. We have many diverse spicy and non-spicy dishes that have attracted Sichuan cuisine lovers. In addition to traditional Sichuan Cuisine, we also serve other delights such as Sichuan cold noodles and scallion pancakes. We invite you to come and try our delicious food!
