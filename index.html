About Us

We are a well established Sichuan Restaurant in the Seattle area. We have many diverse spicy and non-spicy dishes that have attracted Sichuan cuisine lovers. In addition to traditional Sichuan Cuisine, we also serve other delights such as Sichuan cold noodles and scallion pancakes. We invite you to come and try our delicious food!

Specialties

Boiled Fish

What sets traditional Sichuan-style boiled fish from others is its distinct, numbing spice. At Frying Fish, we take that sensation to a whole new level. The mouthful of Sichuan peppercorns and chili makes the tender, fresh fish slices even more flavorful and smooth. It is a delightful dish that we heavily recommend!

沸腾鱼

最不同于水煮鱼的 就是那股麻味了 而沸腾鱼乡的这道菜 真可谓是把麻味做到了极致： 满嘴的藤椒和辣椒味 使新鲜的鱼肉薄片变得更加爽滑入味 让人直呼过瘾！

Cold Noodles

Although our cold noodles have a hint of spiciness, the flavor isn't dominated by the heat. Instead, it's the rich blend of sesame, peanuts, and shredded cucumber that creates a fresh and savory taste. Each layer of flavor truly excites the taste buds!
